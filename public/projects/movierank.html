The Best Movies according to Teresa

My Top 3 Favourite Movies

Formula 1

The visuals are stunning and the music is expressive. Watching this movie with surround audio, give a truly immersive experience. It keeps us on the edge of our seats, especially during the climax of the movie.

Interstellar

My favourite movie directed my Christopher Nolan. It shows the beauty of love transcending space and time. It makes us use our imagination and logical thinking. The characters are well written and executed beautifully.

The Avengers Endgame

One of the best action movies with an amazing plot and story line, accompanied with stunning visual effects and character bonding. The build up from the past sequel movies gives an amazing depth to the Villain.
